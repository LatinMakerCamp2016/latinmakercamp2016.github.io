3D Printing

3D printing is the creation of a real-world, physical model from a 3D design. The process we will be using is called FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling), and it builds 3D objects up from melted plastic layer by layer. In order for this kind of printer to work, the 3D model has to be "sliced" into the individual layers, and then each layer translated into a series of movements for the 3D printer to execute.
