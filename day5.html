Tea Light Programming

The tea lights we are making use a tiny version of an Arduino board called the Trinket - it is much smaller than the Arduino we looked at earlier but has nearly all of the same functionality. We will be using the Trinket to control a NeoPixel - a special kind of easily programmable, multicolor LED. Using the Arduino as a controller, we can make the NeoPixel pulse, dim, or change colors in different patterns.
