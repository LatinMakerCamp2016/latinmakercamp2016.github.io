Additional Maker Resources:

Tinkercad is the primary tool we'll be using to create and modify 3D models.

Thingiverse is a public repository of 3D models that have been created and shared by the community at large. It is a great source for finding inspiration, objects to remix or modify, or creative solutions to problems.
